# Tongxi Wang

👋 Hi, I’m Tongxi Wang. Or @WtxwNs, affiliated with the School of Future Technology at Southeast University.

My research interests lie in AI, learning theory, large language models, and robotics. I am particularly interested in understanding the theoretical foundations of modern learning systems and building efficient, controllable, and reliable intelligent systems.

Recently, I have been working on topics including:
- modeling native parallel reading inside Transformers
- non-stationary reinforcement learning and drift-aware scheduling
- stability and theoretical bounds of in-context learning
- controllable long-song generation with symbolic notation
- evaluation of Chinese-English code-mixed generation in large language models
